Place Cairo and Amiri woff2 font files here. Example:
- Cairo-Regular.woff2
- Cairo-Bold.woff2
- Amiri-Regular.woff2
- Amiri-Bold.woff2

Download from Google Fonts or use https://gwfh.mranftl.com/fonts/cairo and https://gwfh.mranftl.com/fonts/amiri for woff2 files.

After placing, update index.html and styles.css to preload and use these fonts.
